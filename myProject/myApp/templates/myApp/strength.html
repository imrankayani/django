Lose Weight Plan

Here is your weekly workout plan for losing weight:

  • 30-minute cardio (running, cycling) - 3 times a week
  • Strength training (light weights) - 3 times a week
  • Stretching and cool down - after every session
  • Core exercises (plank, sit-ups) - optional extras
Maintain Weight Plan

Here is your weekly workout plan for maintaining weight:

  • 30-minute light cardio (long walk, jogging) - every other day
  • Full-body strength training - 2 times a week
  • Yoga or Pilates - optional extras
  • Stretching and cool down - after every session
  • MAKE SURE TO REFUEL YOUR CALORIES BURNED
Gain Strength Plan

Here is your weekly workout plan for gaining strength:

  • 45-minute weight lifting (compound exercises) - 3 times a week
  • Proggresive Overload! Increase the weight or number of reps on a weekly basis
  • Core strengthening exercises - optional extras
  • Stretching and cool down - after every session
Like this sentence, progress is not always linear. Keep on pushing!
